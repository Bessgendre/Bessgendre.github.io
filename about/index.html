Who Am I

Besides, I am really facinated by the Probability Theory and Stochasitic Processes, not only for its great application everywhere, but also the philosophy of Indeterminism and Free Will standing behind.

About This Website

  This website aims at the publishment of my essays and thoughts. It was finally established in Summer, 2022.

  Some of the posts are required assignments for specific courses. For example, An calculation method modified by symbolic computation in analytical chemistry was written for the final homework of Analytical Chemistry I（2021 Autumn）, and Statistical View for Diffusion is another praised description of diffusive matters.

  Some are notes written when learning some wonderful subjects, such as Quantum Mechanics, Theoretical Mechanics and Statistical Mechanics. There lies lots of insights of mine, and I feel honored if they are benificial for your learning and reviewing.

  Other articles are works about my lab experience: An Introduction to Motility-Induced Phase Separation is a review over the phase separation phenomenon observed in active systems, which is one of the main interests of our group, and Theoretical study on the process of biological phase transition and its physicochemical properties contains my main work when I participated in the college student research program.

  Essays that do not fall into either of the above categories are arguements and philosophical thoughts for shool life and everyday issues. The most satisfying article for me is The Analysis of USTC, where I chaimed that the Truth and Liberty shall become our unshakable belief. Also, The Faults of Zhiyong and The Analysis of Time are two articles that has an impact among the school, in which ancient Chinese was used or philosophical analysis was contained.
